Assassin's Creed Mirage

AAA stealth action Historical setting Single-player

Lore

Assassin's Creed Mirage follows Basim in ninth-century Baghdad, emphasizing a more focused assassin fantasy than broader RPG-heavy entries.

The narrative highlights stealth craft, investigation, and the hidden blade identity at the center of classic Assassin's Creed design.

Gameplay

  • Stealth-forward infiltration with social blending and parkour routes.
  • Target investigation loop that builds assassination opportunities.
  • Historic city design centered on dense urban traversal.
