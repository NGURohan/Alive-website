Baldur's Gate 3

CRPG Turn-based tactics Co-op capable

Lore

Baldur's Gate 3 places players in a mind flayer conflict where character origin, companion relationships, and choice-heavy quests define the narrative outcome.

The story is deeply reactive, with major consequences tied to dialogue, party alignment, and quest resolution paths.

Gameplay

  • Turn-based combat built on Dungeons and Dragons 5e foundations.
  • Extensive roleplay choices with branching dialogue and outcomes.
  • Class-based builds and party synergies drive tactical decision-making.
