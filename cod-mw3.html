Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III

AAA FPS Campaign + Multiplayer Fast-paced combat

Lore

Modern Warfare III continues the reboot-era Call of Duty storyline with high-threat operations and cinematic military set pieces.

Beyond campaign, its core identity is anchored in multiplayer progression and weapon build optimization.

Gameplay

  • First-person shooter gameplay across campaign, multiplayer, and co-op modes.
  • High-tempo gunplay with loadout and attachment customization.
  • Live-service style updates and seasonal progression systems.
