Cyberpunk 2077

Action RPG Open-world sci-fi Story and build-driven

Lore

Wikipedia presents Cyberpunk 2077 as a dystopian story in Night City where the player character, V, becomes linked to the digital engram of Johnny Silverhand.

The narrative combines corporate power struggles, transhuman identity themes, and survival in a brutal megacity ecosystem.

Gameplay

  • First-person action RPG combat with firearms, melee options, hacking, and stealth routes.
  • Character builds are shaped by cyberware, perk trees, weapon archetypes, and mission choices.
  • Open-world structure supports story missions, gigs, side jobs, and faction-style encounters.

Core Systems

  • Buildcraft encourages specialization, from netrunner hacking to high-mobility gunplay or melee.
  • Dialogue and quest choices influence outcomes and relationship arcs.
  • Progression loop balances gear upgrades, augmentations, and narrative advancement.
