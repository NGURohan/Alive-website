Elden Ring

Action RPG Open-world soulslike Exploration + boss progression

Lore

Wikipedia describes Elden Ring as a quest through the Lands Between, where the Tarnished seeks to restore the Elden Ring and become Elden Lord.

The worldbuilding, shaped with contributions from George R. R. Martin, creates a fragmented mythic setting driven by fallen demigods, ruined legacy, and cryptic narrative discovery.

Gameplay

  • Third-person action RPG combat with melee, ranged, magic, summons, and mounted traversal.
  • Open-world exploration with legacy dungeons, field bosses, and hidden questlines.
  • Character builds are highly flexible through stat allocation, weapon scaling, ashes, and spells.

Core Systems

  • High-risk combat loop rewards patience, timing, positioning, and build synergy.
  • Checkpoint and rune systems shape progression, upgrade pacing, and death consequences.
  • World openness allows players to route around difficult encounters and return stronger.
