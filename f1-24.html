F1 24

Formula racing Official licensed Career progression

Lore

F1 24 extends Codemasters' Formula racing line with official teams, drivers, and circuits, focusing on immersion and season progression.

The fantasy is becoming a top-tier motorsport competitor through consistent performance and technical race execution.

Gameplay

  • Licensed Formula 1 and Formula 2 content with official tracks and teams.
  • Career and progression systems for long-form competitive play.
  • Handling precision and race strategy are central to success.
