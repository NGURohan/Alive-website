Final Fantasy VII Rebirth

JRPG Action RPG combat Story-rich

Lore

Rebirth continues Cloud and party's journey after Midgar, expanding the world and deepening character arcs from the Final Fantasy VII remake project.

The game balances cinematic storytelling with broader regional exploration and side activities that flesh out party relationships.

Gameplay

  • Hybrid action-command combat system with tactical party coordination.
  • Large region-based world structure with quests and optional encounters.
  • Character growth through materia, equipment, and synergized abilities.
