Ghost of Tsushima

Action-adventure Open-world samurai drama Single-player + Legends mode

Lore

Wikipedia frames the story around Jin Sakai during the first Mongol invasion of Japan, where he must balance samurai honor with pragmatic stealth tactics to save Tsushima.

The central conflict is philosophical as much as military: tradition versus adaptation under existential pressure.

Gameplay

  • Open-world exploration on foot and horseback across settlements, shrines, and occupied territories.
  • Katana combat with stance switching for different enemy archetypes.
  • Stealth toolkit enables ghost-style approaches for infiltration and targeted elimination.

Core Systems

  • Stance mechanics, timing, and parry windows form the backbone of melee combat depth.
  • Liberation activities and side stories flesh out world-state progression.
  • Legends mode adds cooperative and multiplayer-focused replayability beyond campaign.
