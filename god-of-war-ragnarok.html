God of War Ragnarok

AAA action-adventure Mythic narrative Single-player

Lore

Ragnarok follows Kratos and Atreus through escalating Norse conflicts as prophecy, identity, and father-son tension drive the story forward.

The narrative expands realm-scale stakes while retaining personal emotional focus and character development.

Gameplay

  • Cinematic third-person combat with weapon depth and enemy variety.
  • Exploration across multiple Norse-inspired realms and side activities.
  • Build progression through armor, runic upgrades, and combat specializations.
