Gran Turismo 7

Racing simulation Motorsport focus PlayStation ecosystem

Lore

Gran Turismo 7 emphasizes automotive culture and racing craftsmanship, blending competitive track progression with car collection and tuning mastery.

Its structure is less story-centric and more about driving discipline, machine setup, and motorsport progression loops.

Gameplay

  • Circuit racing with simulation-leaning vehicle handling.
  • Career progression through licenses, events, and car acquisition.
  • Detailed tuning options for setup optimization across tracks.
