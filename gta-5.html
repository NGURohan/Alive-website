Open World Dossier

Grand Theft Auto V

Action-adventure Open-world crime sandbox Single-player + GTA Online

Lore

Wikipedia describes GTA V as a story set in San Andreas, following Michael, Franklin, and Trevor through heists, criminal rivalries, and pressure from powerful institutions.

The multi-protagonist structure lets the narrative jump across different lifestyles and perspectives inside one connected city-state.

Gameplay

  • Third-person or first-person open-world action with driving, shooting, and mission-based progression.
  • Dynamic protagonist switching in single-player across missions and free-roam activity.
  • Large map with heists, side content, businesses, and continuous online expansion.

Core Systems

  • Mission design blends scripted cinematic moments with open-world sandbox freedom.
  • Vehicle, economy, and combat loops feed both story progression and long-term online play.
  • City density and activity variety are key to replayability beyond the main narrative.
