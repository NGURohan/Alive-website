Horizon Forbidden West

Action RPG Post-apocalyptic open world Machine-hunting combat

Lore

Wikipedia identifies Horizon Forbidden West as a sequel where Aloy travels west to stop a spreading biosphere threat in a recovering post-apocalyptic world dominated by machine creatures.

The setting mixes tribal societies, advanced lost technology, and ecological collapse, creating a world where history and survival constantly collide.

Gameplay

  • Open-world questing with ranged and melee combat against distinct machine types.
  • Traversal upgrades expand movement options across vertical spaces and underwater zones.
  • Combat emphasizes weak-point targeting, elemental interactions, and tactical weapon choice.

Core Systems

  • Machine hunting loop ties preparation, tool selection, and encounter execution together.
  • Skill trees and gear upgrades support specialized playstyles from stealth to direct assault.
  • Open-world side content and settlements deepen progression beyond main narrative missions.
