Once Human

Post-apocalyptic survival Open world multiplayer Looter-shooter systems

Lore

According to Wikipedia, Once Human is set in a post-apocalyptic future shaped by the Starfall event, where an alien substance called Stardust transformed the world and its creatures.

You play as a survivor rebuilding in a hostile environment while uncovering threats tied to supernatural pollution, regional strongholds, and monolith-scale boss encounters.

Gameplay

  • Third-person open-world survival with crafting, gathering, base building, and combat.
  • Shared-world structure with scenario-based servers that run on timed cycles.
  • Progression combines looter-shooter combat, stronghold clearing, dungeons (silos), and boss fights.

Core Systems

  • Hunger, hydration, and sanity management directly affect performance and survivability.
  • Memetics unlock skill progression, while Eternaland preserves selected high-end progression between scenarios.
  • Build optimization balances weapon efficiency, survival resources, and scenario timing.
