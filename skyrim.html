The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Open-world RPG Fantasy Single-player

Lore

Skyrim centers on the Dragonborn in a civil-war era province threatened by Alduin, combining prophecy-driven main quests with highly open roleplay freedom.

Players can ignore the main story for long stretches while joining factions, developing skills, and shaping their own character identity.

Gameplay

  • First- or third-person combat using melee, archery, and magic schools.
  • Skill-based progression that levels through use rather than strict classes.
  • Large quest ecosystem with guilds, side stories, and exploration dungeons.
