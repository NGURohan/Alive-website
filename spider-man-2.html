Marvel's Spider-Man 2

AAA action-adventure Superhero open world Single-player

Lore

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 centers on Peter Parker and Miles Morales, combining dual-protagonist storytelling with escalating threats in New York City.

It balances cinematic set pieces with character-driven arcs and villain-focused conflict progression.

Gameplay

  • Open-world traversal with advanced web-swing and movement systems.
  • Fast third-person combat with gadget and ability loadout variety.
  • Mission structure blends narrative moments, side content, and city activities.
