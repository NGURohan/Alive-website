Starfield

AAA sci-fi RPG Space exploration Single-player

Lore

Starfield positions players as members of Constellation exploring settled systems while navigating faction politics, frontier tensions, and cosmic mysteries.

Its setting emphasizes discovery and roleplay freedom across planets, outposts, and ship-based travel loops.

Gameplay

  • First- and third-person RPG systems with questing, shooting, and exploration.
  • Ship customization and space travel integrated into progression.
  • Faction questlines and build choices support different character identities.
