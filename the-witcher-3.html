The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Open-world RPG Story-driven Single-player

Lore

The Witcher 3 follows Geralt of Rivia as he searches for Ciri across war-torn kingdoms, blending political conflict with supernatural threats.

Its narrative strength comes from branching quest outcomes, morally gray decisions, and side stories that often rival main missions in quality.

Gameplay

  • Third-person combat with swords, signs, alchemy, and monster preparation.
  • Dense quest design with branching outcomes and long narrative arcs.
  • Open-world regions with contracts, treasure hunts, and exploration rewards.
