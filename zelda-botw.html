The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Open-world adventure Action-adventure Nintendo exclusive

Lore

Breath of the Wild drops Link into a destroyed Hyrule and gives players broad freedom from the start, making exploration and experimentation central to progression.

The world design rewards curiosity through shrines, environmental puzzles, and emergent combat interactions powered by physics and weather systems.

Gameplay

  • Open-world traversal with climbing, gliding, and systemic environmental interactions.
  • Shrine-based progression loop builds health, stamina, and puzzle mastery.
  • Combat allows weapon variety, rune abilities, and creative problem solving.
